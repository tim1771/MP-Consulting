## Research Summary: Website Requirements for Automotive Consulting Company

Based on current industry best practices, here's what you should provide to a developer/IDE for your automotive consulting website:

***

## **Core Pages \& Information Architecture**

**Essential pages include:**

- **Homepage** - Value proposition, main services, contact info, testimonials
- **Services Pages** - Separate pages for each offering (purchase consultation, maintenance advice, vehicle inspections)
- **Portfolio/Case Studies** - Before/after inspection examples, sample reports, anonymized case studies
- **About Us** - Team credentials, certifications (ASE, manufacturer certifications), professional photos
- **Booking/Contact Page** - Multiple contact methods, appointment scheduling, embedded map
- **FAQ \& Blog** - Educational content, maintenance tips, buying guides, common questions

***

## **Must-Have Features**[^1][^2][^3]

| Feature | Why Important |
| :-- | :-- |
| **Online Appointment Scheduling** | Clients can book consultations and on-site inspections 24/7 |
| **Contact Forms \& Live Chat** | Multiple ways to capture leads and communicate |
| **Mobile-Responsive Design** | Over 50% of traffic comes from mobile devices |
| **Embedded Google Maps** | Show location, service area, directions for on-site inspections |
| **Service Pages with Pricing** | Transparency builds trust; reduces customer anxiety |
| **Customer Testimonials** | Social proof and credibility |
| **High-Quality Photography** | Team, facility, inspection examples |
| **SEO Optimization** | Local keywords so customers can find you ("automotive consultant near me") |
| **Google My Business Integration** | Essential for local search visibility |


***

## **Key Content to Prepare**[^2][^4]

1. **Service Descriptions** - What's included, benefits, typical timeline
2. **Team Information** - Photos, bios, certifications/credentials
3. **Inspection Process** - Step-by-step explanation for on-site visits
4. **Before/After Examples** - Visual evidence of inspection work
5. **Customer Testimonials** - Real feedback from satisfied clients
6. **FAQ Content** - Common questions about purchasing, maintenance, inspections
7. **Educational Blog Posts** - Maintenance tips, buying advice, vehicle care

***

## **Technical Specifications**[^3][^5][^1]

**Design \& UX:**

- Responsive, mobile-first design
- Clean, uncluttered layout (don't overwhelm visitors)
- Intuitive navigation (Services, About, Contact, Book Now)
- Fast loading times (optimize images/code)
- Professional, trustworthy appearance

**Backend Requirements:**

- Secure SSL certificate (HTTPS)
- CRM integration for lead tracking
- Analytics tracking (Google Analytics, conversion tracking)
- Easy content management (update services, testimonials, blog)
- Mobile app consideration for future expansion

**SEO \& Local Search:**

- Local keyword optimization (city-based, "on-site inspections", "vehicle consultation")
- Meta tags, title tags, schema markup
- Consistent NAP (Name, Address, Phone) across all platforms
- Google My Business setup

***

## **Call-to-Actions (CTAs)**[^1][^2]

Place prominently throughout the site:

- "Schedule an Inspection"
- "Book a Consultation"
- "Request a Free Quote"
- "Contact Us Today"
- "Get Expert Advice"

***

## **Additional Features to Consider**[^6][^3]

- **Service area map** - Show coverage zone for on-site inspections
- **Quote calculator** - Estimate service costs
- **Customer portal** - Access past inspections, store reports, book follow-ups
- **Document upload** - Clients submit vehicle photos/info before inspection
- **Email marketing** - Newsletter signups, updates
- **Virtual inspection option** - For phone/video consultations or initial assessments
- **Multi-location support** - If planning expansion

***

## **What to Tell Your Developer**

When briefing an IDE/developer, emphasize:

1. **Target Audience** - Local vehicle owners seeking expert advice (both buyers and current owners)
2. **Primary Goal** - Convert website visitors into booked appointments
3. **Key Differentiator** - You offer professional on-site inspections and personalized consulting
4. **Service Areas** - Specify geographic coverage for on-site work
5. **Growth Plan** - Will you expand locations, add new services, or scale?
6. **Timeline** - When do you need it live?
7. **Budget** - What's your investment range?
8. **Ongoing Support** - Who will manage updates, blog, testimonials?

The website should build **trust, establish expertise, and make booking effortless** for your target customers.[^4][^1]
